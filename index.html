About Tyluxe

At Tyluxe Interior, we believe your home is the most personal canvas in the world. Founded with a passion for beauty and functionality, we craft interiors that speak to your soul — spaces where every detail tells your story.

From opulent living rooms to serene bedrooms and chef-inspired kitchens, our team brings together African craftsmanship and global design sensibilities to deliver environments that are as unique as our clients.

We don't just design rooms — we architect experiences, carefully balancing light, texture, proportion, and material to create homes that feel extraordinary every single day.
